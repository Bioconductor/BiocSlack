Slack channels

Explore Bioconductor Slack channels at https://slack.bioconductor.org/ and join conversations that spark your interest. Our table lists all public channels, updated daily at midnight UTC. It displays the date and time of the last activity - whether it’s a message or a thread reply - alongside a notation indicating how recent this activity was at the time of the last update. Open to everyone interested in Bioconductor, these channels are a great place to participate and collaborate.
